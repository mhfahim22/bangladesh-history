About This Project

Preserving the architectural heritage of Bangladesh

Heritage of Bengal

Heritage of Bengal is a digital archive dedicated to documenting and showcasing the rich historical and architectural heritage of Bangladesh. From ancient Buddhist monasteries to Mughal forts and colonial palaces, our goal is to create a comprehensive resource for researchers, students, and cultural enthusiasts.

Bangladesh is home to an extraordinary diversity of historical sites spanning over two millennia. Yet many of these treasures remain relatively unknown to the wider world. This project aims to change that by providing detailed, well-researched information about the country's most important historical landmarks.

About the Technology

This website is built using Aurora, a modern programming language designed for building high-performance web applications. The backend server is written entirely in Aurora, utilizing its built-in web framework for routing and template rendering. The frontend uses semantic HTML5, CSS3 with custom properties, and minimal JavaScript for interactivity.

The design draws inspiration from the rich visual culture of Bengal, using a color palette that reflects the national identity — deep green, vibrant red, and brass accents set against a warm parchment background.

Featured Sites

Our current collection features eight major historical sites:

  • Sixty Dome Mosque — UNESCO World Heritage Site in Bagerhat
  • Somapura Mahavihara — UNESCO World Heritage Site in Paharpur
  • Lalbagh Fort — Mughal fort in Old Dhaka
  • Ahsan Manzil — Pink Palace of the Nawabs of Dhaka
  • Mahasthangarh — The oldest archaeological site in Bangladesh
  • Mainamati — Buddhist monastery complex in Cumilla
  • Kantajew Temple — Terracotta masterpiece in Dinajpur
  • Panam City — Historic merchant city in Sonargaon

Future Plans

We plan to expand the collection with more sites, including the Sundarbans, the National Parliament Building, and other significant landmarks. We also aim to add interactive maps, virtual tours, and multilingual support (English, Bengali, and Arabic).
